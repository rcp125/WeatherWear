WeatherWear

What is WeatherWear?

WeatherWear is a web application that considers various weather factors to determine the optimal clothing to wear at a specific time at any location. Features include...
- Changing backgrounds according to weather
- Accurate current weather and temperature
- Ability to change from Celcius to Farenheit
- Ability to choose any location via zipcode or city name

How it Works

- Click on the search bar above and enter the location's zipcode or city name. Below, you will see weather statistics as well as a description of what we recommend to wear.
